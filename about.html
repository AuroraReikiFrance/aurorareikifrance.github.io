About

About Aurora Reiki France

My name is Jeffrey Schmidt, and I am a Reiki practitioner based in the town of Le Vésinet, in the country of France. I am fluent in both English and French, with decades of lived in experience in both the United States and France.

More than 9 years ago, I discovered Reiki as a simple and natural way to reconnect, relax, and better manage everyday stress. Over time, I chose to train further in order to extend this beautiful, gentle, peaceful, calming practice to others.

After several years of training and practice, I am now a certified Reiki Master. I currently welcome each person with respect, attentiveness, and kindness in a calm and safe environment.

My Approach

My approach is:

  • gentle
  • non-intrusive
  • respectful of your personal rhythm
  • centered on relaxation and well-being

Each session is a moment for you — without judgment, expectations, or pressure for specific outcomes.

Training & Background

I am a certified Usui Reiki Master, trained according to the traditional Japanese method.

I have taken five instensive Reiki courses and I have thousands of hours of Reiki training and practice.

I continue to deepen my understanding and practice to ensure a high-quality and respectful experience.

My Philosophy

I believe in the importance of slowing down and creating space for rest and self-care in a fast-paced world.

For me, Reiki is a simple tool that encourages relaxation, emotional balance, and inner reconnection.

Important

Reiki is not a substitute for medical treatment or psychological care.

It is a complementary practice focused on relaxation and well-being.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a Japanese energy practice focused on relaxation and overall well-being.

Is Reiki a religion?

No. Reiki is not a religion and does not replace medical care.

Does Reiki cure illnesses?

Reiki does not replace medical treatment.

It is a complementary relaxation practice.

What will I feel during a session?

Each person’s experience is unique.

Many people report deep relaxation, while others may feel very subtle sensations.

How many sessions are needed?

It depends on your personal needs.

Some people come occasionally, others choose regular sessions.

Do I need to believe in Reiki for it to work?

No belief is required to enjoy a moment of well-being.

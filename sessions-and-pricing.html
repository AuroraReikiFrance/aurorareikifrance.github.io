What to Expect During a Reiki Session

Each person experiences Reiki differently.

You may feel:

  • warmth or coolness
  • deep relaxation
  • emotional calm
  • or simply a quiet moment of rest

There is no right or wrong way to experience a session.

Practical Information about Reiki sessions

Session Flow:

  1. Brief conversation at the beginning
  2. Reiki treatment
  3. Short discussion at the end

What to wear?

  • Please wear normal comfortable clothing.
  • You will continue to remain normally dressed all throughout the session.

Un soin de Reiki se passe comment concrètement?

In-person session :

  • lying down on a massage table
  • soft, respectful contact
  • (or hands can be kept at a short distance of a few inches if desired)

Remote session :

  • energetic work done from afar while you stay comfortably at your residence
  • in addition to a video (or audio) call

Important

Reiki is a wellness practice and does not replace medical or psychological care.

What to Expect During a Session

Each person experiences Reiki differently.

You may feel:

  • warmth or coolness
  • deep relaxation
  • emotional calm
  • or simply a quiet moment of rest

There is no right or wrong way to experience a session.

Practical Information

  • Please wear comfortable clothing
  • Allow yourself quiet time afterward if possible
  • Arrive a few minutes early
  • Come without specific expectations

Important

Reiki is a wellness practice and does not replace medical or psychological care.

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a Japanese energy practice that supports relaxation, stress relief, and overall well-being.

It is based on gentle, light hand placement in a calm and respectful environment.

A Reiki session may help you:

  • deeply relax
  • release stress and tension
  • experience a sense of inner calm
  • feel more emotionally centered

Reiki does not replace medical care.

It is a complementary practice focused on relaxation and well-being.
